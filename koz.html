koz

KOZ is inspired by old physical classes equipment used at school physical exercise classes. He has been modernized into a smart system that you can easily arrange at home by adding or deducting drawers. KOZ stands for flexibility, mobility and active way of living. Produced in 3 standards widths for low and high drawer module. You can order entire chest or just 1 drawer unit. Project developed in cooperation with Filip Gałuszka for Jang Furniture.
